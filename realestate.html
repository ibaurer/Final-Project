Expenses In Buying/ Selling an Investment Property
Closing Costs Realtor Commission Time
Commissions are a percentage of sale value. This cost is normally paid by the the buyer. 3% to both agents on both sides of the transaction. Normally, this Commission insures a smooth buying or selling process and provides wisdom via the agent. Closing costs can be a huge expense for the buyer. It is not uncommon on some deals for a first time homebuyer's closing cost to be more than their down payment. An investor is normally putting more down however. These costs are about 5% of the purchase price, depending on a lot of factors Time is an often unaccounted for expense when searching for and purchasing an investment property. If a deal falls through, you and your agent or team are back to square one. For this reason it is important to be careful to have a great team and solid offers.
