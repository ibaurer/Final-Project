My Favorite Deviled Eggs

Ingredients: 12 Hard Boiled Eggs, 1 cup Mayo, 1/4 cup mustard, 1 teaspoon cayenne

  1. Step 1: If you haven't boiled your eggs yet, please do so now
  2. Step 2: Cool and Peel the eggs
  3. Step 3: Cut the eggs lengthwise, and scoop out the yolk into a bowl
  4. Step 4: Mash up egg yolks
  5. Step 5: combine mayo, mustard, and cayenne into the egg yolks
  6. Step 6: Spoon yolk mixture evenly into the empty egg whites
  7. Step 7: Serve and Enjoy!




Best Ever Pie Dough

Ingredients: Ice water, 1.5 cups flour, 1 stick butter This simple but incredible recipe is adapted from Erin Jean McDowell
  1. Step 1: Make sure the ice water is chilling
  2. Step 2: Cut the butter into 1/4in chunks
  3. Step 3: Toss flour and butter together
  4. Step 4: Use a butter knife or your hands to cut the butter into the flour in pea sized pieces
  5. Step 5: Add ice water until dough comes together
  6. Step 6: Wrap dough in plastic wrap and chill in the fridge 30 minutes
  7. Step 7: Use according to your favorite pie recipe!
